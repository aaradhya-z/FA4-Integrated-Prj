Ila Sachani- Summary

Ila Sachani was born into a farmer's family in the quiet settings of Amreli, Gujarat.

From the beginning, life present her its challenges as she was born with her hands hanging loose by her sides.

She could not use her hands to draw figures in the sand, pluck wild flowers or use her them in the usual way.

Ila's mother and grandmother were single-minded and skilled in the traditional art of Kathiawar embroidery.

They encouraged Ila and helped her to overcome her challenge. They decided to share the art with Ila and taught her to create many beautiful patterns using her feet!

Threading needle was hard,but Ila did not give up and became an expert at a young age. Her elder brother and sister helped her too. She learned many styles, even ones from outside Gujarat-Kachhi, Lacknawi, and others

For Ila, embroidery became a way to express herself. She crafted fine desings for her family and freinds, who were awestruck by her talent.Soon the people in the village discovered Ila's talent and admired her work. Soon word reached the government office, in Surat.They were impressed by Ila's story.

Some samples of her embroidery were displayed at the state exhibition. People admired and purchased her creations. Ila became no more but famous!

Encouraged by this popularity, Ila participated in more exhibitions within the state and at the national level. She recieved many awards honours for her art, willpower and creativity that her work showed. She also became a celebrated artist and reciever of the President's Medal. She proved through her creations that challenges can be turned victories with focus, hardwork, positivity and never giving up. Her embroidery created with her feet, reminds us that art hes the power to rise above physical bounderies and touch the soul.
